ABOUT THE ARTIST

Merci Ewuresi is a multidisciplinary visual artist and researcher of Austrian and Ghanaian descent. She employs a range of artistic mediums including painting, collaging, videography, and photography. Her work explores themes of connectivity, womanhood, and cultural heritage. Interconnection of all beings, nature, and space are always at the back of her mind. Further drawing from decolonial and Black Anarchist feminist theory she explores multiple ways to implement that within her art.

ANIMATION

STREY!

'Strey!' is a short animation that visualizes a selection of iconic moments in the historic Black emancipation movement. The animation highlights a timeline in history, taking you from the 14th century West African Coast, up until the 2020 Black Lives Matter movement.

DATE: September 2021

Project by: Rossel Chaslie and Merci Ewuresi
